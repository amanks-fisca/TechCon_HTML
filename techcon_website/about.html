Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots effort to connect technology leaders and startups. Over the years, it has evolved into a global event attended by innovators from every corner of the industry.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to empower people through technology by fostering collaboration, sharing knowledge, and exploring the future of innovation. We believe in accessibility, sustainability, and the positive potential of tech to change lives.
